About me

Hi there, my name is Hristina, a 27 years old girl with love for cats (and other animals, yes :D).

My origins are from North Macedonia, but for the last 4 years I've made a new home in Germany, in the cozy city of Freiburg. I'm a curious person by nature and love to learn about varieties of topics. But, in particular I developed passion for computers and how they work, so I frequently spend my nights tinkering with my linux system, changing keybindings of my tiling window manager (i3) or obsessively designing the polybar to sit juuuust right.

As I dwelled deeper, I've decided to work towards the goal of transforming that passion into a profession, with hope that I will find opportunities which will allow me to learn more and use that knowledge practically.
